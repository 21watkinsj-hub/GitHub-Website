About Me

Hi! My name is Jeremiah, some of my favorite hobbies include, playing basketball, watching movies and eating.

Basketball is definitely one of my favorite hobbies. I first started playing in 5th grade for my school team. I didn’t get much playing time in middle school or high school, but I always enjoyed the game. Basketball became a release for me — a way to relieve stress and clear my mind. I haven’t been in the gym as much lately, but I plan on getting back in there and improving my skills.

I think the lack of playing time came from a lack of confidence. I always felt inadequate, even though I was actually one of the best players on the team. If I had gotten out of my own head and just played, I know I would have earned more minutes. I still play here and there, and I’m working on learning how to dunk. Dunking is something I’ve put off for years. I’ve been able to grab the rim since 8th grade, and yet I’m still not dunking — but I’m getting closer.
